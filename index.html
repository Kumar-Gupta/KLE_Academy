BACHELOR ALLIED HEALTH SCIENCE

Medical technologists and those in allied health sciences make valuable contributions to patient care by performing clinical laboratory procedures and lend hand in emergency care. Make a successful career in Allied Health Science as a Medical Technologist or an expert in emergency care with courses crafted to lend the edge of technology in diagnosis and in cutting edge critical care. Join KAHER’s Allied Health Science courses offered us in our campuses across Karnataka, and arm yourself with the latest in health science technology and fine tune your skills in your path towards adding time to lives and care.
